Cell Membrane

Hello! I am The Cell Membrane, but my friends call me Plasma Membrane; anything will do. It's a thin, flexible structure made primarily of a phospholipid bilayer, with proteins and carbohydrates embedded within. This structure gives the membrane its semi-permeable nature, allowing the cell to regulate what enters and exits while maintaining a stable internal environment.
